Who is Virat Kohli?

Virat Kohli is a world-class cricketer known for his dedication, passion, and leadership. Born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, he has become a legend in the cricket world.

Career Highlights

  • Former captain of Team India
  • Over 80 international centuries
  • 2011 Cricket World Cup winner
  • Holds multiple batting records

Why Fans Love Him

Virat’s fearless attitude, hard work, and sportsmanship make him a role model for millions of cricket fans worldwide.
